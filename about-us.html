Company Profile

Established in 1996, Surecool Appliances has been a trusted name in providing air coolers and fans for homes and businesses across India. With nearly three decades of experience, we have built our reputation on quality, durability, and customer satisfaction. Our products are designed to deliver powerful cooling with energy efficiency, making them a reliable choice for both residential and commercial spaces. From desert coolers to compact fans, we offer solutions that combine innovation with affordability. At Surecool, we believe in creating lasting value by focusing on excellence, honest pricing, and long-term relationships with our customers. For over 25 years, we’ve been committed to keeping India cool—one appliance at a time.
